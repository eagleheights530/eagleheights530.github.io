About Just Poker

Just Poker is an elegant companion for serious poker players, featuring comprehensive tools to analyze your game and improve your strategy. Designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, our app helps you make better decisions at the poker table.

Features

  • Comprehensive Poker Equity Calculator to analyze winning chances in any scenario
  • Hand History Tracker to record and analyze your poker sessions
  • Beautiful card designs with multiple style options
  • Customizable card backs for a personalized experience
  • Clean, intuitive interface designed for serious poker players

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the equity calculator work?
Enter your cards, opponent ranges, and community cards to calculate your winning chances. The calculator uses advanced algorithms to determine equity across thousands of possible outcomes.
Does the app save my hand history data?
Yes, all hand history data is stored locally on your device. Your poker session data never leaves your phone.
How can I customize the app appearance?
Go to the home screen and select from available card styles and back designs to personalize your experience.
What poker variants are supported?
Currently, Just Poker supports Texas Hold'em with plans to add more variants in future updates.
