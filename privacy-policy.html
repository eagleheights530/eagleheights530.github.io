Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Thank you for using Just Poker. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle your information.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information or user data. Just Poker is designed with your privacy in mind, and we do not gather, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about our users.

Local Data Storage

All hand history data and app settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We do not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting tools that would collect usage data from your device.

Third-Party Services

Just Poker does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@justpoker.app.
